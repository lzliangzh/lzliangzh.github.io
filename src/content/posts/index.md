## Projects

[Hybrid Watermarking](https://github.com/lzliangzh/Hybrid-Watermark) -- Generative AI 2025 Fall Final Project. Tree-Ring/RingID excel at robustness to geometric transforms, while Gaussian Shading preserves latent statistics and avoids quality loss. Hybrid-Watermark fuses these strengths by embedding Gaussian Shading and RingID in different latent channels and combining their detection results.
